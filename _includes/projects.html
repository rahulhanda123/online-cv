Projects

An extensive list of projects I have worked on over the years and links to write ups about some of them. You can also have a look at the github repo for the ones which are public.

Emotion Recognition - Recognizing emotions in real time webcam feed. The project consisted of recognizing facial features in the video and then predicting the emotions on a trained ”fisherfaces” recognizer.
Traffic Sign Recognition - Traffic Sign recognition on the GTSRB dataset. LeNet inspired neural network is fed with pre processed data(resizing, contrast normalization). Randomly distorted images are added to the dataset to increase the training data.
Neural Style Transfer on videos - Extracting low level features like the colour and texture from one image and applying it to more semantic, higher level features of each video frame.
Atom - A comprehensive website template solution for startups/developers to market their mobile apps.
Delta - A responsive Bootstrap one page theme designed to help app developers promote their mobile apps
